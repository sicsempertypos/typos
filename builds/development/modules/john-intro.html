This is the row wrapper

Your story must be told. It is epic. It is profound. It is like nothing ever written. But your grammar is atrocious. Your spelling is dreadful. Your prose is lacking. Your story is one for the ages, but you do not have the words with which to express it.

I do.

I know the tale you are trying to tell. I see the story laid out before me in vivid detail. And I have the power to transform your lifeless text into the flowing, emotional, Shakespearean masterpiece your storyline deserves!

An avid reader and lifelong perfectionist, John has recently discovered his true passion: correcting other people's mistakes.

In all his prior work, he never could resist pointing out the little grammatical errors all around him, and after years of resisting the urge, he has finally succumbed to the inevitable and embraced his inner Grouchy Grammarian.
